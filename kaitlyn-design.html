Materials and Finishes Palette

Wood Flooring

Brand: Hallmark Floors

Color/Style: Warm Beige Oak, Smooth Finish

Justification: Warm beige oak wood flooring provides a neutral and versatile base that pairs well with a variety of color schemes. The smooth finish adds a clean, modern look, while the beige tone complements the rest of the palette, adding warmth and sophistication.

Area Rug

Brand: Shaw Floors

Color/Style: Cream with Olive Green and Gold Accents, Subtle Geometric Pattern

Justification: The cream rug with olive green and gold accents introduces subtle color and pattern into the space, tying together the warm and cool tones. The geometric pattern adds a modern touch without overwhelming the design, and the soft cream base helps to brighten the space.

Paint Color 1

Brand: Sherwin Williams

Color: Accessible Beige

Justification: Accessible Beige is a warm neutral that provides a soft, inviting backdrop for the lobby. It enhances the natural light and pairs beautifully with the warmer tones in the wood flooring and rug. This color is versatile and works well with both traditional and modern elements.

Paint Color 2 (Accent Wall)

Brand: Behr

Color: Olive Green (Secret Meadow)

Justification: The olive green accent wall brings a touch of nature into the space, creating a calm and serene atmosphere. This deep green adds depth and richness to the lobby, contrasting nicely with the beige walls and enhancing the overall palette. The earthy tone grounds the design, making the space feel more connected to the natural environment.

Drapes

Brand: Kravet

Style/Color: Cream Linen Drapes with Black Trim

Justification: The cream linen drapes with black trim add a touch of elegance to the lobby. The cream color maintains a light and airy feel, while the black trim provides a crisp, modern contrast. These drapes soften the space and frame the windows beautifully, offering both privacy and light control.

Sofa Upholstery

Brand: Knoll

Color/Material: Charcoal Gray, Textured Fabric

Justification: The charcoal gray sofa introduces a strong, grounding element to the space. The textured fabric adds depth and interest, making the sofa a central piece in the lobby. The dark gray color contrasts with the lighter tones in the flooring and walls, creating a balanced and cohesive look.

Side Chair Upholstery

Brand: Maharam

Color/Material: Olive Green with Gold Accents, Patterned Fabric

Justification: The olive green chairs with gold accents complement the accent wall and rug, tying the color scheme together. The pattern adds visual interest and elevates the overall design, while the olive green color brings a natural, calming element to the seating area.

Pillow Upholstery

Brand: ArcCom

Color/Material: Mix of Solid Black and Gold Patterns

Justification: The pillows in solid black and gold patterns add a luxurious touch to the seating arrangement. The black pillows provide contrast and sophistication, while the gold patterns introduce a subtle touch of glamour. These pillows create a layered, dynamic look, enhancing the overall comfort and style of the lobby.
