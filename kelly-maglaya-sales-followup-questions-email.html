Answers to Your Questions

Hi Kelly,

It was great speaking with you earlier. As promised, here are the answers to the questions you asked about Course Creator 360:

1. App Customization and Push Notifications

Can I get a custom app with Course Creator 360? What customization options are available?

Yes, you can get a custom-branded app for an additional $60 per month on top of your subscription. Alternatively, you can use the gray-labeled app called Kollab (K-O-L-L-A-B) without customization.

Do push notifications work with module drips?

Currently, we are not sure if push notifications can be scheduled with module drips. We will get back to you with an answer soon.

Can I use Afterpay as a payment option for students?

Yes, you can integrate Afterpay through an API, available on both the Elite and other plans. We can help you set this up during your onboarding call.

2. App Features and Functionality

Is there a resume playback feature for videos?

Yes, users can resume their videos exactly where they left off within the Kollab app.

Does the app support widgets, like affirmation widgets on the home screen?

Yes, there are affirmations in the app but you cannot custom create your own widgets as of now.

3. Gamification and Community

Is there gamification in the platform?

Yes, CC360 offers gamification within the community feature. Users earn points as they complete courses, and you can set up rewards tied to these points, such as free coaching sessions or merchandise.

Can you set different levels of access within the community?

Yes, you can create different access levels within the community and use the community space to upsell courses or other offerings.

4. App Functionality for Sales

Can I promote additional courses through the app?

Yes, the mobile version of the app will feature and promote other courses users can purchase. However, it might require users to leave the app to make purchases, which helps avoid in-app fees.

5. Pricing and Support

Can I downgrade my plan after using Done-For-You services?

Yes, you can downgrade your plan at any time, as our subscriptions are month-to-month with no contracts. The Done-For-You services available in the higher-tier plans are flexible and can be adjusted as needed.

What does support look like for top-tier plans?

Top-tier plans, like the Elite Plan, offer enhanced support. This includes priority customer service and onboarding calls where our team assists with everything from payment processor setup to email deliverability.

6. Content and File Hosting

Is there a limit on file sizes for uploads?

No, there is no specific limit on the size of your uploads. You can store as much content as you want on the platform.

Can I host freebies and digital downloads for unpaid users?

Yes, CC360 allows you to host free content, such as digital downloads or sneak peeks into courses, even for users who are on a free trial.
