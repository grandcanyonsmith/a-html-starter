Summary of Onboarding Call with Kelly - Life Coach Client

Business Info:

Current Operations:

  • She is a life coach and a stay-at-home mom.
  • Wishes to transition her coaching business to an online platform.
  • Aims to create courses that teach people how to coach themselves.

  • Intends to build an app with features like push notifications, habit trackers, and affirmation widgets.

Goals:

  • Integrate features such as push notifications aligned with module drips.
  • Include habit trackers and journal entries for clients to track their progress.
  • Implement affirmation widgets on clients' home screens.
  • Utilize gamification elements like points and rewards to motivate clients.

Course Creation:

  • Create online courses hosted on Course Creator 360.
  • Offer digital downloads, freebies, and sneak peeks into other courses.
  • Provide assessments and assignments within modules.
  • Allow for upsells and downsells within the platform.

Needs/Problems:

  • Technical Support: Feels overwhelmed by software stacks and needs assistance with setup.
  • Platform Features: Has specific questions about Course Creator 360 capabilities.
  • Customization: Wants to ensure the app meets her unique requirements.
  • Time Management: Desires to avoid spending excessive time learning new software.
  • Subscription Plans: Interested in higher-tier plans that offer more support.

Homework/Action Points:

  • Start a free trial of Course Creator 360 to test platform capabilities.
  • Explore the Collab app to understand the end-user experience.
  • Consider purchasing Course Creator Pro at the discounted rate of $300.
  • Await responses regarding app features:
    • Push notifications scheduling with module drips.
    • In-app purchases and Apple/Google store policies.
    • Affirmation widgets and custom app widgets.
    • E-signature capabilities for forms.
    • Resume playback functionality for video content.
  • Decide on the subscription plan based on the level of support needed.
